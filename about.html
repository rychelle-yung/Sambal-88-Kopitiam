About Sambal 88 Kopitiam

Welcome to Sambal 88 Kopitiam, where tradition meets taste. Established in 1988, we began as a humble family-run business in the Old Klang Valley, serving our community with passion and dedication. Over the years, our commitment to quality and authenticity has fueled our growth, leading us to relocate to a larger space in Bandar Sunway in 2016.

Our kopitiam is more than just a place to enjoy great food; it's a reflection of our heritage and a testament to our journey. From our iconic sambal recipes to our warm and welcoming atmosphere, every visit to Sambal 88 is a celebration of our story and our flavors.

Join us as we continue to offer the best of traditional kopitiam delights and create new memories with every meal.
