Frequently Asked Questions

What are your restaurant hours?

Monday - Friday: 11AM - 2:30PM | 5PM - 9PM (Last call 8:40PM)

Saturday & Sunday: 12PM - 3PM | 5PM - 10PM (Last call 9:40PM)

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian options on our menu. Please check our menu for details.

Can I make a reservation?

Yes, you can book a table through our "Book a Table" page on the website or by calling us directly.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for events of all sizes. Whether it's a small gathering or a large celebration, we can create a customized menu to suit your needs. Please contact us directly to discuss your event and get a quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and e-wallets such as Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, ShopeePay, and Alipay. For online orders, you can also pay through secure online payment methods.

Can I purchase gift cards for Sambal 88?

Yes, gift cards for Sambal 88 are available for purchase. You can buy them in-store or through our website. They make a great gift for anyone who loves our food!

Are there any ongoing promotions or discounts?

We frequently have promotions and discounts! Check our website or follow us on social media for the latest updates on special offers and deals.

Is there parking available at Sambal 88?

Yes, we offer parking for our customers. There is ample parking available in the vicinity of our restaurant. If you have any questions about parking, please feel free to ask our staff.

Can I bring my own wine or beverages to Sambal 88?

We have a selection of beverages available at the restaurant, and we kindly ask that you refrain from bringing your own alcoholic beverages. If you have any specific requests, please contact us beforehand.

Are pets allowed in the restaurant?

While we love animals, we ask that pets do not enter the restaurant to ensure the comfort and safety of all our guests. Service animals are always welcome.

Do you have high chairs or booster seats for children?

Yes, we have high chairs and booster seats available for young children. Please let us know when you arrive so we can prepare one for you.

What should I do if I left something behind in the restaurant?

If you’ve left something behind, please contact us as soon as possible. We’ll check our lost and found and get in touch with you if your item is found.
